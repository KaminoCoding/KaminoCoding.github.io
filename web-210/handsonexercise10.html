Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Flour
  • 1 cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup of White Sugar
  • 2 sticks of Butter, softened
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 tsp of Baking Soda
  • 1 bag of Chocolate Chips
  • 2 Large eggs

Making

  1. Pre-heat oven to 325° Farenheight
  2. Place butter, white sugar, and brown sugar in mixer and mix until it becomes a paste
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla and mix
  4. Mix in the baking soda
  5. Stir in the flour
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Place on cookie sheet and cook for 10-14 minutes
